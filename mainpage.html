Interesting facts

Sleep deprivation

Drunk brain

Being awake for 18 hours affects your brain like having a 0.05% blood alcohol level. It slows your focus, reaction time, and decision-making.

Bad sleep

Hurts your heart

People who lack sleep have a 48% higher chance of developing heart disease. Poor rest increases blood pressure and stress in the body.

Less sleep

More anxiety

Sleeping less than 6 hours a night can lead to anxiety and depression. Your brain needs rest to process emotions and stay mentally balanced.

Memory

Drops

Sleep deprivation can reduce your ability to learn and recall information by up to 40%. Staying up late to study might actually make you forget more.
